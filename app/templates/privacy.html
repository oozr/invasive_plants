Privacy

Short note on how this site handles analytics.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors use the site (for example which pages are viewed and roughly where traffic comes from). Google processes this information according to its own policies.

Google Privacy Policy · Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on

Account access

When you request access, we store the name, email address, organization, and reason you provide so administrators can review the request.

For approved accounts, we also store account status, admin review details, one-time login token metadata, and login timestamps needed to operate access control.

Changes

We may update this page occasionally. The “Privacy” link in the site footer always points to the current text.
