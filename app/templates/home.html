Project Description

This database brings together official records of plant species regulated as invasive plants, noxious weeds, or related biosecurity concerns across regional, national, and international jurisdictions. It is designed to support clearer comparison of invasive-plant regulations across countries for research, agricultural decision-making, policy analysis, and public reference.
